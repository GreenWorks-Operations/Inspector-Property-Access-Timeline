Here are some need-to-know details for the field
▾

Once you are active in the field, your MLS is almost always your fastest and most direct resource for access-related issues. 📋 GreenWorks Teams Communication Guide

General Access Issues

  • Contact your MLS directly — they can provide access without needing to route through the RSC team as a middleman
  • RSC handles your initial access setup, but once you are on-site your MLS is the fastest point of contact. If you run into issues, do not go silent — drop a message in your AE channel so the RSC team stays in the loop

CBS Code Needed On-Site

  • Call your MLS and provide the serial number on the lockbox
  • They can generate a CBS code on the spot — this is often faster than contacting the listing agent directly
The serial number is on the small display window on the side of the lockbox (circled). Read that number to your MLS to get your CBS code.

RSC Team

  • RSC is responsible for handling your initial property access request before you arrive on site
  • If you have an issue before arriving, start with RSC — if you are already on-site, go directly to your MLS
Bottom line: your MLS is your best resource in the field. Save your local MLS member services number in your phone so you can reach them quickly when it matters.
